Publishing

Last updated: July 28, 2025 1 min read

The Explore page is your direct link from developer to user. To publish your work there, you'll need to follow a few steps.

Our publishing system ensures users can access developer content without worrying about quality or safety. We review for issues such as:

  • Malicious code, exploits, or harmful intent
  • Unoptimized code or performance problems
  • Content with bugs or issues that hinder the user experience
  • Improper or missing resource disposal
  • Failure to respect user privacy and data security
  • Not following our application guidelines

If you are unsure about something, you can always ask our discord!

The Verification Process

Our verification process ensures your content is not only safe for users but also developed to the highest possible standards. We manually review all submissions to make sure they meet our criteria. When you submit a request, you'll be prompted to upload your source code, title, description, and other necessary details.

Once your request is submitted, we'll review it and get back to you as soon as possible. You'll receive a confirmation email when your request has been received.

You'll get one of two replies:

  • Approved: Your content has been approved! You'll receive a confirmation email asking you to agree to the terms and conditions and confirm publication.
  • Rejected: Your content has been rejected. This could be because it doesn't meet our standards or for another reason, which we'll explain in detail. Please make any necessary changes, and then you can resubmit.
