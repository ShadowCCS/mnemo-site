Contact Support

Last updated: July 28, 2025 1 min read

Currently, the only way to get support by us directly is by visiting our discord server.

Here you can ask questions, get help with your problems, or just chat with other users, if the issue is severe enough, you can go to the "Support" channel and ask for help there.

However, most of the time you can find the answer to your question in the the documentation, but sometimes things might not work as expected.
